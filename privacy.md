# Privacy Policy for Buttekha

**Last updated:** 23 June 2026

Buttekha ("the App") is provided by DELV PTY LTD ("we", "us"). This policy
explains what information we collect through the App, how it is used, and
your rights regarding that information.

## Who we are

**Data controller:** DELV PTY LTD
**Contact:** privacy@delv.org

## Information we collect

The App is designed to work without requiring you to create an account.
However, to provide and improve the ripeness-detection feature, we collect
the following data each time you use it:

- **Audio recordings.** When you tap a watermelon, the App records a short
  audio clip of the tap.
- **Photographs.** When you photograph a watermelon, the resulting image is
  stored.
- **Prediction metadata.** The verdict (ripe / not ripe), the confidence
  score, and (if you provide it) your feedback on whether the verdict was
  correct.
- **Anonymous user identifier.** A randomly generated identifier issued by
  Firebase Authentication that lets us group sessions from the same device
  without identifying you personally.

We do **not** collect: your name, email, phone number, location, contacts,
device identifiers other than the anonymous ID above, or any data from other
apps on your device.

## How we use this information

- To run the ripeness analysis on each watermelon you scan.
- To improve the accuracy of our prediction model over time, by reviewing
  patterns in collected recordings and verdicts in aggregate.

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share it with advertisers or
data brokers. We do **not** use it to track you across other apps or
websites.

## Where your data is stored

All collected data is stored in Google Firebase services
(Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Cloud Storage)
operated by Google LLC. Servers are located in the United States
(us-central1 region). Google's processing of this data is governed by
[Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## How long we keep it

Audio recordings, photographs, and session metadata are retained for
analysis purposes. You may request deletion of all data associated with
your anonymous identifier at any time (see below).

## Your rights

You have the right to:

- Request a copy of any data we hold linked to your anonymous identifier.
- Request deletion of all data linked to your anonymous identifier.
- Withdraw consent for future data collection (uninstalling the App stops
  all future collection).

To exercise these rights, email **privacy@delv.org** with a request and
the anonymous identifier you wish to act on. (You can find your identifier
inside the App's settings, if exposed in a future version; otherwise
contact us and we will help locate it.)

## Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
data from children. If you believe a child has used the App and we should
delete their data, contact **privacy@delv.org**.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be
announced in the App. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the
most recent change.

## Contact

Questions about this policy or about your data: **privacy@delv.org**
